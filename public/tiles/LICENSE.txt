Basemap tiles in this directory
================================

Source data: Natural Earth (1:50m Cultural/Physical Vectors, "land" theme)
https://www.naturalearthdata.com/downloads/50m-physical-vectors/

License: Public Domain
https://www.naturalearthdata.com/about/terms-of-use/

  "All versions of Natural Earth raster + vector map data found on this
  website are in the public domain. You may use the maps in any manner,
  including modifying the content and design, electronic dissemination,
  and offset printing. The primary authors, Tom Patterson and Nathaniel
  Vaughn Kelso, and all other contributors reserve no rights and no
  attribution is required."

No attribution required; safe for commercial use.

These PNG tiles are not distributed by Natural Earth directly — they were
rendered from Natural Earth's public-domain land-polygon vector data by
this project's own script, scripts/render_tiles.py. That script (and its
rendering) inherits the same public-domain status as the source data.

Note: Natural Earth's public-domain status is unconditional for the
"land" theme used here. A small number of other Natural Earth themes
(Admin-1 boundaries, Marine Boundaries) carry additional licensing
caveats outside the U.S. due to third-party data dependencies — those
themes are not used by this project's tiles.
